### How it Works
When you register a biometric device, your browser generates a unique public-private key pair. The private key never leaves your device's secure enclave (like Touch ID or Face ID), and the public key is stored on our servers.

### Benefits
- **Unphishable**: Since there's no password to steal, phishing attacks become impossible.
- **Frictionless**: Sign in in milliseconds with a simple touch or glance.
- **Secure**: Even if our database is compromised, your actual biometric data is never stored on our servers.
